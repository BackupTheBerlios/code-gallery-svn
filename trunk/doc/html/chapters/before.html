Please read carefully!! Requirements

Code-Gallery have some fondamental requirements:

  • Python 2.4 or higher
  • ImageMagick 6 or higher
And if you want to use the GUI the extra requirements are:
  • GTK+ 2.6 or higher
  • PyGTK 2.6 or hogher
  • libglade 2 or higher
